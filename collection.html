My Collection

My sister, my niece and I have tried to collect all the Disney princess VHSes and DVDs. Along with that we have a Scooby Doo collection because they're still making movies to this day and it was our childhood dream to collect them all. We also have a bunch of movies we fell in love with so we got them on DVD. Finally we collect series like Pretty Little Liars, Twilight Saga and others.

In the future, we hope to grow our collection and get more series like Criminal Minds.

My sister grew up with a bunch of CDs so I continued that collection when she passed them down to me. Then I had an unhealthy obsession with Andrews McMeel Publishing. I tried to get my hands on as many poetry books as I could.
